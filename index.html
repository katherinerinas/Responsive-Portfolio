About Me

I obtained my undergraduate degree at the University of Kansas. I studied abroad in Ireland for a semester and played basketball for University College Cork. I then went for a year of Medical Laboratory Science school at a hospital in Kansas City, Missouri.

I relocated to Denver, Colorado, where I worked for National Jewish Health for three years before moving to Boulder Community Health for one year. The laboratory offered a great learning experience, but I am excited about the creative possibilities offered by the world of web development.
